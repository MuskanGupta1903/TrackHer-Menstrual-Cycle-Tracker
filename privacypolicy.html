Privacy Policy

Welcome to TrackHer! We are here to commit to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly.

Our Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. Please take a moment to review it, as it helps you understand our approach to protecting your privacy while using our services.

We may collect personal details like your name, email address, and any information you enter into the app. This helps us tailor your experience.

Additionally, we may collect data on your interactions with the app, such as your activity logs and preferences, which helps us improve our features and services.

We use your information to provide features, improve performance, and offer personalized support. Your data helps us enhance your experience and allows us to send you helpful notifications or updates related to your cycle tracking.

We also use your information for internal analytics to monitor app performance and user engagement, ensuring we continue to provide a valuable and efficient service.

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure access to protect your information from unauthorized access. We regularly review our security procedures to ensure your data is protected at all times.

We also limit access to your personal data to authorized personnel only and provide regular training on data protection to our team members.

We use cookies for site analytics and improved usability. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Cookies help us improve our website by tracking how users interact with it, making it more intuitive.

Our cookies also allow us to remember your preferences, such as language settings, for a more personalized browsing experience.

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Once you leave our site, we encourage you to read the privacy policy of any third-party site you visit.

Some third-party services, like analytics tools, may also collect your data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their terms before using these services.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We recommend reviewing it from time to time for any changes. Significant updates will be communicated to you via email or through a notification on our platform.

We strive to keep our users informed about how their data is being used, so you can feel confident in your privacy choices with us.

If you have questions about your privacy or how your data is handled, you can reach out to us via our Contact page or email us directly at support@trackher.com.

Our team is always here to assist you and address any concerns regarding your personal data and privacy rights.
